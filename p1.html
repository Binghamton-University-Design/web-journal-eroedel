ILIZTRATIONS

Elizabeth Roedel

Visual Message for the Inhabitants of Other Celestial Bodies

For this project, I wanted to communicate the relationship between humanity and our environment on Earth. My message to celestial beings is to demonstrate that human advancement in technology is slowy destroying our planet. The tree icons represent the degredation of nature over time in an upward motion. The first icon symbolizes a peaceful relationship with nature whereas the second icon and third icons represent our drastic shift to technology and finally the degradation of nature. The exclamation point on the phone screen is to show how this requires immediate attention. The phone displays the different stages of our environment as a result of technological advancement. I wanted to place emphasis on our constant use of technology through a third and first person perspective. There is also some irony that plays into this idea that the phone is the object that is showcasing this destructive cycle. Lastly, I added a small diagram of our solar system to communicate where we are located in relation to outer space.
