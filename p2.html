Exquisite Corpse

For this project, my partner and I used the guidelines for the surrealist exquisite corpse game where we both designed and inserted our own images to create an abstract form. Our vision was to create an abstract human form that was not limited to animals, inanimate objects, or layering. We decided to each design one vertical half of the body. I was responsible for the left side of the body while my partner designed the right side. After we each put together our images, we implemented each other's images into html and css pages with the use of a css grid.
